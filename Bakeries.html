    The Ultimate Guide to London's Best Bakeries

1. Nordic Bakery

Nordic Bakery is famous for its traditional cinnabon buns. Enjoy the hot, crispy and aromatic buns with a cup of coffee. The prices for all their products are affordable too. The bakery can be found in Covent Garden, Soho, Marylebone and Baker Street.
Find out more about Nordic Bakery here: http://nordicbakery.com

2. Peggy Porschen

Who doesn't know the beautiful, extremely instagrammable entrance of Peggy Porshcen? The cakes are also as pretty as their entrance, and taste really good too! They are good for kids, and also for adults who are feeling the young vibes! Peggy Porschen only has one bakery that is located in Victoria.
Check out Peggy Porschen's wide range of menu here: https://www.peggyporschen.com

3. Dominique Ansel

Beautifully glazed cronuts, blossoming hot chocolate and cookie shots with vanilla milk! What else can you ask for? Enjoy the finest range of pastries and drinks from World's Best Pastry Chef 2017. Dominique Ansel Bakery can be found in Victoria, London, as well as several other locations in New York and Tokyo.
For more information about the bakery and the chef, go to their website: https://dominiqueansel.com

4. Rinkoff's Bakery

Looking for the best crodoughs and Danish pastry in London? Why not head directly to Rinkoff's Bakery, located in Stepney and Bethnal Green? Their 107-year-long history and experience in baking will not let you down.
Learn more about Rinkoff's legacy here: http://www.rinkoffbakery.co.uk
